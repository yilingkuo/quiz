Movie Quiz App

Hi there, welcome to the movie filming quiz game!
Let me guide you to where to start!
account_circle First, click this icon and enter your user name.
my_location This button tracks your location.
theaters Click here to open some filming location questions.
After selecting an answer from the popup window, click 'submit your answer'.
Scroll to the bottom of the app to see submission result:
--If the answer is submitted successfully, the result will show 'answer row is inserted'
--If not, it will show 'the data has mistakes'. When submission fails, please restart the HTTP server.
